Madi's Famous Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Indulge in our soft, fluffy cinnamon rolls, swirled with a rich and sweet brown sugar butter mixture that will melt in your mouth, releasing a warm, comforting aroma that's sure to transport you to a cozy bakery on a cold winter morning.

Each tender bite is infused with the perfect balance of sweet and spicy, making them an irresistible treat that's sure to become a new favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cube butter (room temperature)
  • 1 1/2 cubes butter (room temperature)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tbsp yeast
  • Brown sugar (to sprinkle)
  • Cinnamon (to sprinkle)

Instructions

  1. Warm the Milk: Heat 1 cup of milk to 120°F (or follow the temperature instructions on your yeast packet).
  2. Prepare the Dough Mixture: In your mixer bowl, combine:
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 cube room temperature butter
    • 1 egg
  3. Combine Milk and Ingredients: Once the milk reaches the appropriate temperature, add it to the mixture in the bread machine bowl.
  4. Add Flour and Yeast: Add 3 1/4 cups flour and 1 1/2 tbsp yeast to the bread machine bowl.
  5. Start the Bread Machine: Run your bread machine through two cycles of rises (approximately 55 minutes total).
  6. Prepare the Cinnamon Rolls:
    • After the dough has risen, roll it out.
    • Spread 1 1/2 cubes of room temperature butter evenly onto the rolled-out dough.
    • Sprinkle the dough generously with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Roll and Slice: Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut it evenly into 12 pieces.
  8. Prepare the Baking Pan: Place the 12 rolls in a greased 9x13 pan.
  9. Final Rise: Allow the rolls to rise for 30 minutes.
  10. Bake: Bake the rolls at 350°F for 30 minutes, turning the pan halfway through (15 minutes per side).
