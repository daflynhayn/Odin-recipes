Venison Spagetti

Description

This recipe for Smoked Venison is a perfect blend of rich flavors and tender texture. The venison is slow-cooked in a sweet and tangy BBQ sauce, resulting in a dish that's both delicious and impressive.

With its fall-off-the-bone tenderness and finger-licking good flavor, this Smoked Venison recipe is sure to become a favorite among your family and friends.

Ingredients

Per lb of Venison

  • Brown Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Sea Salt: ~1.25 tablespoons
  • Sesame Oil: 0.25 tablespoon
  • Garlic: 1/8 clove (or round up for taste)
  • Star Anise: ~1/8 bag (or 1 whole star anise)
  • Ginger: A small pinch (~1/4 teaspoon)

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Trim venison into 3/4" strips.
  2. Combine: Add salt and sugar to the meat, ensuring even distribution.
  3. Mix: Add garlic, ginger, star anise, and sesame oil to the mixture.
  4. Marinate: Soak the venison strips in the marinade for 24-48 hours in the refrigerator, stirring every few hours to ensure even coating.
  5. Smoker: Remove the strips from the marinade and smoke on low heat for 1 hour.
  6. Serve: Enjoy your flavorful smoked venison!
