Venison Spagetti

Description

Tangle up your taste buds with our classic spaghetti, tossed in a savory blend of olive oil, garlic, and herbs that dances across the palate, leaving behind a satisfying crunch from perfectly cooked al dente noodles.

Every bite is a masterful balance of tender textures and deep, rich flavors, evoking the warmth and comfort of a homemade Italian feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Garlic salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 bottle of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground venison

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Spaghetti Noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
    • Add 1 tablespoon of salt to the boiling water.
    • Add the box of spaghetti noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
  2. Cook the Ground Venison:
    • In a large saucepan, brown the ground venison over medium heat.
    • Season the meat with garlic salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Prepare the Sauce:
    • Add 1 can of tomato sauce and 1 bottle of spaghetti sauce to the cooked and seasoned meat.
    • Stir well and simmer on low heat.
  4. Balance the Flavor:
    • Add 1 tablespoon of sugar to the sauce to reduce acidity.
  5. Serve:
    • Combine the cooked spaghetti noodles with the sauce or serve the sauce over the noodles.
